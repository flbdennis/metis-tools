## Understanding Exchange Rates

Exchange rates constantly fluctuate based on:

- **Supply and demand** — driven by trade, tourism, and investment
- **Interest rates** — higher rates typically strengthen a currency
- **Inflation** — lower inflation generally means a stronger currency
- **Political stability** — uncertainty can weaken a currency

## Tips for Getting the Best Rates

1. **Always check the mid-market rate** before exchanging money — it's the "real" rate before markups
2. **Avoid airport and hotel exchanges** — they often charge the highest fees
3. **Use a no-foreign-transaction-fee credit card** for international purchases
4. **Compare services** — Wise, Revolut, and traditional banks each offer different rates
5. **Time large transfers** — rates can shift significantly within a week
