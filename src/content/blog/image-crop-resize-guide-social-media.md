## Pro Tips for Image Cropping

1. **Use the rule of thirds** — mentally divide your image into a 3×3 grid and place key subjects at intersections
2. **Leave headroom** — for profile pictures and portraits, leave space above the subject
3. **Check your platform** — each social network displays images differently in feeds vs. expanded view
4. **Maintain aspect ratio** — never stretch images; always crop or resize proportionally
5. **Save at the right resolution** — don't upscale (it creates blur) — only downscale or crop
