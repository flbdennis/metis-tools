Let’s be direct: MetisTools shows advertisements. I know ads can be annoying, but they are the only reason this site can stay **100% free** for everyone.

## The Cost of Running a Free Site

Many people assume that running a website costs nothing. That’s not true. Every month, I pay for:

- Domain registration (`metistools.com`)
- Cloudflare Pages (free tier is generous, but I eventually need paid plans for advanced features)
- API services (some free tiers are limited; I may need paid keys for better reliability)
- My own time (thousands of hours building, testing, and supporting the site)

I’ve chosen **not** to charge users, sell your data, or lock essential features behind a paywall. The only sustainable way to cover costs without sacrificing privacy is **display advertising**.

## How Our Ads Work

We use **Google AdSense**, a trusted advertising platform. Here’s what you can expect:

- **No pop‑ups or interstitials**: Ads never cover the tool you’re using.
- **No auto‑play videos**: Only static banners or text ads.
- **No tracking beyond what Google normally does**: Google uses anonymized cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting [Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).
- **No data shared with me**: I never see your personal information. Google handles everything.

## Why Not Just Ask for Donations?

Donations are unpredictable. Some months I might get $100, other months $0. With ads, the income is small but steady – enough to pay the bills and justify the time I spend improving the site.

I also believe that a voluntary “tip jar” works for content creators (YouTubers, podcasters), but for a tool site, most users simply won’t donate. Ads are a more reliable model that keeps the service free for everyone, including those who can’t afford to donate.

## How You Can Support MetisTools

Even if you don’t click ads, there are many ways to help:

1. **Whitelist this site in your ad blocker** – This is the single biggest help. If you use an ad blocker, adding `metistools.com` to your allowlist ensures I still earn a tiny amount from your visit.
2. **Share the site** – Tell your friends, colleagues, or social media followers about MetisTools. Word of mouth is priceless.
3. **Provide feedback** – Bug reports and feature suggestions make the site better for everyone.
4. **Consider a one‑time donation** – If you really hate ads and want to support directly, I may add a “Buy me a coffee” link in the future. (Not yet, but possibly.)

## A Promise

I promise that:

- **Ads will never be intrusive**. If they become so, please call me out.
- **I’ll never sell your data**. Your privacy is non‑negotiable.
- **The core tools will always be free**. No bait‑and‑switch.
