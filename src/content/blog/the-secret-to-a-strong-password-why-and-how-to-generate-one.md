# The Secret to a Strong Password: Why and How to Generate One

Using `123456` or `password` as your password is like leaving your front door unlocked. **Weak passwords** are the number one cause of account breaches.

## What Makes a Password Strong?

A strong password should be:

- **Long** – at least 12 characters.
- **Complex** – mix of uppercase, lowercase, digits, and symbols.
- **Random** – not dictionary words or personal info.

## Why You Need a Password Generator

- **Avoid predictable patterns** – Humans are bad at randomness.
- **Unique per account** – Never reuse passwords across services.
- **Protect against brute force** – Randomness makes guessing impossible.

## Password Management Tips

- Use a **password manager** (like Bitwarden, 1Password) to store them.
- Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** wherever possible.
- Change passwords only when a breach is suspected – frequent changes lead to weaker ones.
