## What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin text derived from Cicero's "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (45 BC). It's been the printing industry's standard dummy text since the 1500s. The familiar opening — "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..." — has been used for centuries because it:

- **Looks natural** — letter distribution similar to English
- **Doesn't distract** — readers focus on layout, not content
- **Is universally recognized** — clients know it's placeholder

## Best Practices for Placeholder Text

1. **Replace before launch** — never ship with Lorem Ipsum in production
2. **Use realistic lengths** — match placeholder length to expected real content
3. **Consider accessibility** — screen readers will read Lorem Ipsum aloud if you're testing
4. **Switch to real content early** — actual copy often reveals layout issues hidden by dummy text
