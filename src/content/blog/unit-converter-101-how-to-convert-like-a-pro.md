### Quick Reference: Length

- 1 inch = 2.54 cm
- 1 foot = 0.3048 m
- 1 yard = 0.9144 m
- 1 mile = 1.609 km

### Quick Reference: Temperature

- Celsius to Fahrenheit: `(°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F`
- Fahrenheit to Celsius: `(°F − 32) × 5/9 = °C`
